## Why no LICENSE file?

Under international copyright law (including the Berne Convention), original works are protected by
copyright from the moment of creation — no registration or explicit license is required. The absence
of a LICENSE file therefore defaults to "all rights reserved by the copyright holder."

We have chosen to make this position explicit via this NOTICE.md rather than relying on the absence
of a file, because:

- Clarity reduces ambiguity for team members, contractors, and third parties
- It signals an intentional decision, not an oversight
- It satisfies auditor expectations for internal proprietary repositories

This repository is a private internal scaffold. It is not open source and is intended solely for
internal use by the team cloning and extending it. No redistribution, forking, or derivative works
are permitted outside of `<ORGANIZATION-NAME>` without explicit written authorization.
